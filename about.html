More About Our Mission

The divers range of engineering products being manufactured by Mukesh Earthmovers & Contractors are covering a wide range of pollution control equipment, industrial cleaning equipment and air pollution control equipment which attract customers from all industrial segments such as steel plants, Aluminum plants, Cement plants, Power plants, Coal handling plants, Ports, Mines etc. On the other head, the civic equipment range covers Municipal Corporations and service providers for solid waste handling and management.
